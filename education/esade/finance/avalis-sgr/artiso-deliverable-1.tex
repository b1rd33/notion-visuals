\section{Executive Summary}

Fashion design workflows today are fragmented across disconnected tools, making them slow, expensive, and hard to scale. Existing AI tools are generic and do not understand brand-specific design language or workflows, which limits adoption inside companies.

ARTISO addresses this by building an AI operating system that learns a brand's visual DNA---silhouettes, colors, materials, style rules---and applies it across the entire creative workflow, from moodboards to production-ready CAD drawings. The platform is built on a proprietary four-layer fashion ontology and brand memory system, which creates switching costs that compound over time. The target customers are design and creative teams at fashion brands, from mid-size companies to large global groups. The company was founded in May 2024 by a team from Mango with deep fashion and AI expertise.

ARTISO has secured paid pilots with Deichmann, TATA Group, and NF\&TA, generating over \euro{}60\,k in pilot revenue to date. More than 200 brands are on the waitlist for platform access. Early pilots show approximately 2 hours saved per design, 20--30\% sampling cost reduction, and 15--25\% fewer revision cycles.

The business model is a seatless B2B SaaS combining an annual platform fee, a one-off setup fee, and usage-based AI credits. Annual contract values range from \euro{}7--40\,k for SMEs to \euro{}40--300\,k for enterprise clients. The company is raising a \euro{}3\,M pre-seed round to fund product development (60\%), sales scaling (25\%), and operations (15\%) over 18 months. The financial model projects ARR growing from \euro{}1.8\,M in FY2027 to \euro{}29.3\,M by FY2030, with EBITDA breakeven expected in Q2 2028.

% ── SECTION 3 ───────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{Previous Rounds of Financing}

No prior external funding. ARTISO has been bootstrapped by the founders, generating early revenue through paid enterprise pilots. The current \euro{}3\,M pre-seed is the company's first institutional raise, targeting a lead VC plus strategic angels with an 18-month runway.
